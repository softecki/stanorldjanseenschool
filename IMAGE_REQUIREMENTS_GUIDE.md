## 🎨 Image Style Guidelines

### Overall Style
- ✅ **Professional** - Clean, well-lit, high quality
- ✅ **Authentic** - Real students, natural poses
- ✅ **Positive** - Happy, engaged, confident expressions
- ✅ **Diverse** - Mix of boys and girls, different ages
- ✅ **Uniform** - Students in clean school uniforms (white shirts, ties, dark bottoms)
- ✅ **Modern** - Contemporary school settings
- ✅ **Bright** - Good lighting, colorful but professional

### Uniform Details
**Standard School Uniform:**
- White or light blue shirts
- School ties (blue/burgundy)
- Dark pants for boys
- Dark skirts for girls (knee-length)
- Black shoes
- Optional: Blazers with school crest

### Settings to Capture
1. **Classrooms** - Modern, bright, with desks and blackboards/whiteboards
2. **Library** - Bookshelves, reading areas, study tables
3. **Science Labs** - Lab equipment, microscopes, experiments
4. **Sports Facilities** - Fields, courts, students in sports wear
5. **Campus Grounds** - School buildings, gardens, walkways
6. **Assembly Halls** - Large groups, formal events
7. **Graduation** - Caps, gowns, certificates

## 📐 Image Size Recommendations

### Banner/Slider Images
- **Dimension:** 1920x1080px or 1920x700px
- **Format:** WebP (for web optimization)
- **Quality:** High (80-90%)
- **Orientation:** Landscape

### Square/Portrait Images
- **Dimension:** 800x800px or 800x500px
- **Format:** WebP
- **Quality:** High (80-90%)
- **Orientation:** Square or Portrait

### Icons
- **Format:** SVG (vector, scalable)
- **Size:** 24x24px to 64x64px
- **Colors:** Match school theme (blue, gold)

## 🔍 Where to Source Images

### Option 1: Professional Photography
✅ **Best Option** - Hire a photographer to capture real Nalopa School students
- Schedule a photo day
- Get parent/guardian consent forms
- Capture various school activities
- Professional quality guaranteed

### Option 2: Stock Photos
Sources for African student images:
- **Unsplash** (unsplash.com) - Free, high-quality
- **Pexels** (pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- **Shutterstock** (shutterstock.com) - Paid, extensive library
- **iStock** (istockphoto.com) - Paid, professional
- **Adobe Stock** (stock.adobe.com) - Paid, high quality

**Search Terms:**
- "African students classroom"
- "African school children uniforms"
- "African students science lab"
- "African students graduation"
- "African school campus"
- "Tanzania students school"
- "East African students learning"

### Option 3: AI-Generated Images
- **Midjourney** - High-quality AI images
- **DALL-E** - OpenAI image generator
- **Stable Diffusion** - Open-source AI

**Example Prompt:**
```
"Professional photograph of smart African students in white school 
uniforms studying in a modern, bright classroom in Tanzania, 
natural lighting, high quality, realistic, diverse group"
```

## 🎯 Quick Tips

1. **Consistency** - Use images from the same photo shoot or with similar style
2. **Authenticity** - Real students > stock photos > AI
3. **Quality** - High resolution, well-lit, professional
4. **Optimization** - Compress without losing quality
5. **Testing** - Check on mobile, tablet, desktop
6. **Backup** - Keep original high-res versions
